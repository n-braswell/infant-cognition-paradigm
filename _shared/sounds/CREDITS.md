# Sound credits

All clips are from Kenney (https://kenney.nl) audio packs, released under
Creative Commons CC0 1.0 (public domain). No attribution is legally required;
this file is recorded as good practice.

Packs used: Digital Audio, RPG Audio, Impact Sounds, Music Jingles.
Fetched from the Godot-addon mirrors under github.com/Boyquotes and
github.com/Calinou (verbatim CC0 copies).

Files are named `<beat>__<label>.ogg`; `<beat>` is the design.html TIMELINE
beat the clip is a candidate for. The chosen clip per beat is set in the
SOUND_MAP object in design.html.
